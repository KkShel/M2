The ship currently known as the Nemesis Mi’ithir was once one of the Alliance’s Frontier Exploration ships, the Aqua-4. An engineer of theirs, Mi’ithir, was deemed unfit for service and terminated, her body taken to be recycled into materials to feed other workers. The remaining crew went rogue, mutinying against any officers that still answered to a government that valued productivity over life.

The Aqua-4 was officially decommissioned after its crew defected, no longer registered but still found sailing under a different name and deliberately sabotaging the Alliance’s expansion efforts. The ship now runs on an eight-person skeleton crew. Two members who joined after their decommissioning are Distif and Hirmithi, a small-planet pilot going off on her own and a robot being hunted for “stealing” her own body.

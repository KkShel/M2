Setting!

Uhust
Our protagonist, Mykse-Binheso, is from the planet Uhust, home of the Conbrynys.
The Inter-System Alliance
Far from Uhust, there exist a multitude of star-systems with their own inhabited planets and various sentient species.
The Outer Rim
This ominous title refers to the areas of space that exist at the edge of the Inter-System Allianceâ€™s authority. This is the frontier, the new world, largely uninhabited and newly colonized. Anything beyond the Outer Rim is unknown and barely explored. The communities within it largely exist to evade the watchful eyes of the law.1
